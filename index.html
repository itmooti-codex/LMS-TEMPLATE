The blueprint for your children’s book

You’ll discover how to write memorable stories for middle-grade readers. That’s generally readers between eight to thirteen years old.

Books in this age group are very popular and have seen a surge in popularity in recent years. It’s an age when young readers are curious, they love to learn and they enjoy reading about younger protagonists they can relate to. It’s also an age where you can start exploring more complex topics, take your characters on epic adventures and have fun with humour.
